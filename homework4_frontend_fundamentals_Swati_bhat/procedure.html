Steps to make Ras Malai😋

  1. Boil 1 liter of milk.
  2. Curdle milk with 2 tablespoons of lemon juice or vinegar.🥣
  3. Strain paneer using a muslin cloth. Rinse and squeeze out excess water.
  4. Hang paneer for 30 minutes to drain🫨
  5. Knead paneer for 10 minutes until smooth. add 1 tablespoon of semolina/flour (optional)🤍
  6. Shape paneer into small discs.👻
  7. Boil 4 cups of water with 1 cup of sugar.
  8. Cook paneer discs in boiling syrup for 15 minutes; let cool.👻
  9. Boil 1 liter of milk and reduce to half.
  10. Add 1 cup of sugar, cardamom, saffron, and nuts to the milk; simmer for 5️⃣ - 7️⃣ minutes.
  11. Transfer paneer discs to milk syrup; soak for 2-3 hours (refrigerate for best results).
  12. Garnish with nuts and saffron; serve chilled.😋🤍
